Game Settings & Rules

The grid contains 2 types of cells: alive (bright) and dead (dark)

When starting a new game, a randomized grid is created, with the percentage of living cells as defined below.

Next, the simulation begins, in turns.

Each turn, each cell will either remain in its present state, or change its state according to the following rules (these are the default rules of Conway's game of life, but can be modified):


Living cells

will continue living in the next step if they have more than and less than neighbours, otherwise, they die

Dead cells

will revive in the next step if they have more than and less than neighbours, otherwise, they remain dead
